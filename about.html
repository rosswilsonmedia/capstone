About Capstone

The Capstone course is designed to allow graduating students to demonstrate the skills they have learned over the course of their education here at Butler University. Students are encouraged to take risks as they work on bringing a project to fruition throughout the entire semester, either in collaboration with other students or individually. Below you can find out more about each of the students in the Capstone course and discover more of their work.
