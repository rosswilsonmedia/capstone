Capstone Projects

The Capstone course is all about students creating projects of their own, either in collaboration with one another or individually. These projects are the result of months of hard work, and we can't wait to share them with you. Below you can find out more about each of the projects created in Capstone this semester, their creators, and where you can find them.

No Place To Go

No Place to Go is an original song written and recorded by Luke Kite. It features a live studio band and was recorded at Aire Born Studios in Zionsville, Indiana. The song is off his upcoming album.


03.25.2017

03.25.2017 is the first song Kealy Welage has written and recorded. This song is about love and more specifically, a relationship forming, growing and the path it is on.
Lyrics by Kealy Welage, Music by Cutler Armstrong, and Mixed and Recorded by Gavin Zawacki

More or Less with Jess

In addition to helping out with artwork for Kealy's song, 03.25.2017, and helping with the Capstone Virtual Event, CJ Robinson has spent the semester creating a podcast on the Clubhouse App called "More or Less with Jess."

Capstone Virtual Event

Meghan Jones and Ross Wilson have spent the semester figuring out how to create the Capstone Virtual Event! This included interviewing classmates, gathering footage, creating graphics, editing the virtual stream, and creating this website to highlight the wonderful projects that students have taken on this semester! Additional graphic help was provided by CJ Robinson.

Butler Jazz Live Sessions

The Butler Jazz Live Sessions are a compilation of music from three of Butler's top jazz groups. The sessions were recorded live on campus at Butler's very own recording studio. Gavin Zawacki was the head producer, sound engineer, and mixing engineer on the project.

The Escape from My Other Self

This project is a compilation of my pure emotions throughout the past semester. It was my escape from the ups and downs, the good and bad. During this time I've been exploring what makes me, well - me, and this music is a reflection of my growth.


Learn more about Bryan Kwoun

The Openly Taciturn by Eclectic Music Services

This project is Payton Wesley's second full album in which he explores the more internal aspects of himself.

Shy

Maria Diebolt has spent the semester recording her new song entitled Shy and a cover of Toxic by Britney Spears. She has been working with Bella Nasser to grow her social media presence through the creation of graphics and promotional materials.

Coping Music Video

As a Digital Media Studies student in the Music Industry capstone, it seemed only natural that my project be a music video. While searching for a (copyright-free) song to craft a music video around, I stumbled upon and was instantly drawn to "Coping". This instrumental song by Butler Alum John Verdico was included on the Butler Music Industry Association Sampler Vol. 3, which is a CD of student made songs, made in 2015.
